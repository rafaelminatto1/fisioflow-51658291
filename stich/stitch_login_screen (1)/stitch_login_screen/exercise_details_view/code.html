Shoulder Abduction

4.5 (120 reviews) | Beginner

This exercise strengthens the deltoid muscles and improves shoulder mobility. It's commonly used in rehabilitation programs for shoulder injuries and to enhance overall upper body strength.

Details

Indications

Shoulder pain, rotator cuff injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, and general shoulder weakness.

Contraindications

Acute shoulder injuries, dislocations, severe pain, or any condition where movement is restricted by a healthcare professional.

Equipment

Resistance band or light dumbbell.
